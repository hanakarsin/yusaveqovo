LaToya Ward - Age, Family, Bio

Actress who has landed roles in such films as Miss Congeniality (2000) and on shows like House of Cards. She is also an Instagram model and comedian with more than 410,000 followers. 

Before Fame

She lived in San Antonio, Texas before moving to Los Angeles, California. She attended Texas State University and Webster University. 

Trivia

She won Best Lead Actress honors at the London Film Festival for her performance in the film Lost (2018). 

Family Life

Her mother's name is Helen. She has a sister named Michelle. 

Associated With

She has made Instagram videos with people like Kenyatis Jones.
