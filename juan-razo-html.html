Juan Razo - Age, Family, Bio

Creator of the Mexican-American radio and television personality Don Cheto and the host of El Show de Don Cheto found on Channel 62 in Los Angeles. He could also be heard on the radio station East Los FM in the video game Grand Theft Auto V. 

Before Fame

He graduated from El Monte High School. He worked in a factory where he folded children's pajamas. 

Trivia

He based his famous Don Cheto character on a man he knew from La Sauceda. He worked as an intern at La Que Buena. 

Family Life

He is an immigrant from a Michoacán farm town. He arrived in the United States in 1996. His father started a strawberry jam business. 

Associated With

As Don Cheto, he released a music video parody of Psy's "Gangnam Style" which featured cameos from Ana Bárbara and Jenni Rivera.
