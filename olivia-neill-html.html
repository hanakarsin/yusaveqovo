Olivia Neill - Age, Family, Bio

Northern Irish YouTube star who primarily posts lifestyle and beauty videos to her self-titled channel. She has gained over 750,000 subscribers on her channel. She has collaborated with the fashion company MOTEL. 

Before Fame

She originally posted to her YouTube channel in August 2016. 

Trivia

Associated With
