Woman Who Danced Creatively with Packs of Chewing Gum at Parties to Feed 7 Kids Seeks Help

- A woman has been sighted washing away her worries at a Lagos party

- Maymunat Aminat dances creatively with packs of choco Milo and chewing gum to fend for herself and her kids

- The 40-year-old divorcee is seeking the help, saying she presently caters for seven children

A 40-year-old divorcee, Maymunat Aminat, has been sighted creatively dancing to musical songs with some packs of choco Milo and chewing gum arranged on her head in the Ebute-Metta area of Lagos state.

The woman is always seen at different kinds of ceremonies within the local communities on the Mainland and Lagos Island part of the state dancing with those products she sells.

Speaking with Legit.ng, TUKO.co.ke's sister site in Nigeria, on Monday, May 24, Aminat narrated that she had been doing the bold show as a market strategy due to financial constraint even though she loves dancing.

The woman stated that she dances through the areas of Campos, City Hall, Agboole Oya, Agboole Etti, Freeman, Adams Rica and some other places in the area of the state.

Aminat, with 7 children and no husband, explained that she left her former hubby because of his philandering attitude and neglect for the upkeep of the children.

The woman, therefore, appealed to Nigerians to help her with accommodation as she is currently sleeping under a building at Lagos Island.
