Crossdresser, James Brown Reveals Why He Acts Like A Woman

Popular Nigerian Cross Dresser, James Brown has revealed the reason why he acts and behave like a woman as a man. 

Naija News reports that James Brown took to his Instagram page to share a photo of himself rocking a male clothing with the caption: ‘Meet the king of Africa.’

Reacting to the photo, a curious fan identified as Itodyou_6996 wrote: ‘You fine as a man i don’t know why you’re acting like a woman.’

Replying to the fan, James Brown noted that he acts like a woman because it pays the bills.

”Because it pays the bills naturally. Being a woman can make you wealthy if you are smart. I can teach you,” he said.

My Mother Infected Me With HIV

In 2020, James Brown opened up about living with the HIV virus in an interview with BBC Igbo, stating that he knew about his status when he was 12 years old.

According to him, learning about his status left him broken, but he accepted his fate and decided to live with it as he had no option.

James noted that he got HIV through mother-to-child transmission and has been living with it for a long time
