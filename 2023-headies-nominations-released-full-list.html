2023 Headies Nominations Released [Full List]

The Headies on Wednesday released the much anticipated nominations list for its 16th edition.

Naija News reports that Damini Ogulu, a Grammy award-winner popularly known as Burna Boy, received the most nominations on the list with a total of 11 nominations.

He also battles for the Album of the Year with Davido, Rema, Asake, Omah Lay and Victony.
