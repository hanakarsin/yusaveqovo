Biz Betzing - Age, Family, Bio

Internet personality, model and actress who has posted photos from her photo shoots on her self-titled Instagram account. She was featured in the HBO hit series Shameless and has modeled for companies like Banjanan. 

Before Fame

She grew up in Michigan. She began posting to Instagram in August of 2016.

Trivia

In 2019, she was featured in a short film called Stuck in Temporary and a feature called Long Lost Sister. She has been a brand ambassador for the J80 Fit athletic wear company. 

Family Life

She is the sister of popular YouTuber Katie Betzing. She has another sister, and a brother as well.

Associated With

She has a cousin named Landen Betzing who has appeared in her sister Katie's videos.
