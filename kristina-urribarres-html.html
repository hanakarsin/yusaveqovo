Kristina Urribarres - Age, Family, Bio

Beauty and fashion YouTuber who has earned a large fanbase through her makeup tips, hair tutorials, product reviews and hauls posted to her YouTube channel and Instagram under the username: K3chocolate. She is also a model and dancer. 

Before Fame

She grew up in Kiev, Ukraine and spent a year in Cuba as a child. She moved to the United States at age 12 and went on to attend Granby High School, graduating in the same class as actor Grant Gustin. She earned a Bachelor of Science degree from Old Dominion University in 2012. 

Trivia

She can be seen in several music videos, including Jolley Brothers' "Empty Halls" and Provoke, Destroy's "Cutthroat." She was one of six squad members to represent cruelty-free, Los Angeles-based skincare company DermaE for 2020 and 2021. She is a fitness instructor on the TurboFit app, alongside other instructors such as Cory Wharton from Real World.  

Family Life

Her father is Cuban and her mother is Ukrainian. Her grandfather, Ruben Urribarres Pérez, was a famous Cuban orchestra director for over 40 years and toured with the National Orchestra. She has been based in Los Angeles, California. 

Associated With

She once posted a makeup tutorial inspired by Rita Ora's look in the music video for Iggy Azalea's "Black Widow."
