Kim Shantal - Age, Family, Bio

Social media star and dancer who has earned more than 11 million Instagram followers by sharing high-quality modeling photos as well as snapshots from her life as a go-go dancer. She also interviews various entertainers and people from her life on her self-titled YouTube channel.  

Before Fame

She worked for several years a cheerleader, backup dancer and model. She went to school at Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México in Mexico City, where she studied gastronomy. 

Trivia

She participated in the "Ruleta" game on Ryan Hoffman's YouTube channel. In 2019, she was presented with an MTV MIAW award for God Level Instagrammer - Mexico. She is a member of the Fénix Team influencer group. 

Family Life

Her full name is Kimberly Shantal Adame. Her mother, Alicia Parra, named her after the pink Power Ranger. She has two younger brothers named Brian and Brandon. She was in a relationship with popular YouTuber Malcriado. 

Associated With

She appeared in Badabun challenge videos with fellow social media stars Andrea Zúñiga and Carolina Díaz.
