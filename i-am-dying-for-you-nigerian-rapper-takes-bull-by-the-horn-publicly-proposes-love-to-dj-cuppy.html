'I Am Dying For You' Nigerian Rapper Takes Bull By The Horn, Publicly Proposes Love To DJ Cuppy

Self-acclaimed king of rap, Vic O has proposed his undying love to Nigerian disc jockey, DJ Cuppy after she said she’s still single while Rihanna is now pregnant.

This comes after DJ Cuppy, reacted to popular singer and entrepreneur, Rihanna’s pregnancy.

Rihanna had earlier in the week confirmed she’s expecting her first child with her rapper boyfriend A$AP Rocky.

Rihanna, in the pictures, was seen showing off her growing baby bump as A$AP Rocky kissed her forehead.

Reacting, DJ Cuppy, in a tweet via her Twitter handle on Monday, said she is still single while Rihanna is pregnant.

“Rihanna is pregnant and I’m still single,” She tweeted alongside an upside-down face emoji.

Reacting to her statement, Vic O confessed his love for her, adding that he would propose to her without wasting time if she gives him a chance.

“Short note to you my queen to be.. @cuppymusic, I’m very much available for you babe.. You Gon’ love ma style..

“I’m a cool dude you know.

“You know i’ve been in love with ya for long time..

“And if we’ve met her now i will have proposed to you without wasting time..

“Bebe .. I’m very serious, i really like alot.. like i’m dying for you,” Vic O wrote.
