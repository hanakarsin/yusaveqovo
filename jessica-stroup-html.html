Jessica Stroup - Age, Family, Bio

Award-winning actress who was most recognized for her role on CW's remake of 90210. She also appeared in the 2008 film Prom Night.

Before Fame

She made her acting debut in a guest-starring role on Nickelodeon's Unfabulous in 2005.

Trivia

She played a small role in the 2012 comedy film Ted.

Family Life

She grew up with a brother named Dave. She dated her 90210 co-star, Dustin Milligan for two years before the couple ended their relationship in August 2010.

Associated With

She co-starred with Rob Estes on 90210.
