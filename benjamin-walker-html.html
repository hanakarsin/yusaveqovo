Benjamin Walker - Age, Family, Bio

Played the protagonist in the film Vampire Hunter. His roles in Flags of Our Fathers and Kinsey bolstered his fame.

Before Fame

He attended Julliard where he studied acting.

Trivia

He was in the Broadway musical Bloody Bloody Andrew Jackson .

Family Life

He married actress Mamie Gummer in 2011; but the couple divorced in 2013. He married actress Kaya Scodelario in 2015. They welcomed a son the following year. Their second child was born in January 2022. 

Associated With

He was in the film The War Boys with Peter Gallagher.
