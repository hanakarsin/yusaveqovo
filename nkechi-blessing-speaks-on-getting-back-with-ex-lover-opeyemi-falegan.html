Nkechi Blessing Speaks On Getting Back With Ex-lover, Opeyemi Falegan

Nollywood actress, Nkechi Blessing Sunday has reacted after a follower insinuated that she is back with former lover, Opeyemi Falegan after a visit to Ekiti.

Naija News Recalls that the actress and Falegan had gotten into the act of washing their dirty linens in public after their relationship ended.

Falegan had called Nkechi’s personal hygiene into question, accusing her of not changing her underpants for days. This was followed by a comeback from Nkechi stating that she does not wear underpants.

In a recent development, the actress visited Ekiti State, which happens to be the state her ex-lover, Opeyemi Falegan hails from.

She took to her Instagram page to share some videos from the moment alongside a caption that reads:

“OGO AGBAYE. THE ONE WHO LIGHTS UP ANYWHERE SHE STEPS HER FEET. THANK YOU, EKITI TILL NEXT TIME. GOOD EVENING LAGOS. #UNSTOPPABLE #OGOAGBAYE”

Reacting to the post, a netizen identified as damilola_olatunji wondered if the actress was back with her man.

She wrote: “Is she bk with her man”

In response, Nkechi Blessing who wasn’t pleased with the comment terribly lambasted the lady for leaving such a remark.

“You go like collect swear this early morning? Una own life no pass man” she replied.
