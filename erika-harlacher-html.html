Erika Harlacher - Age, Family, Bio

American voice actress known for work on popular video games and anime shows. She's best known for voicing Elizabeth Liones in The Seven Deadly Sins and Ann Takamaki in Persona 5.

Before Fame

In 2008, she started attending California State Polytechnic University, Pomona, where she studied theatre. She began her voice acting career with the role of Keiko Ida in the anime TV series K-On!

Trivia

She's voiced many characters over the years, including Kurapika in Hunter x Hunter and Sadira in the video game Killer Instinct.

Family Life

She married Luke Stone in October 2020, with whom she has a dog named Gus. She also has a younger sister named Natalie.

Associated With

She said she was inspired to pursue voice acting after watching an interview with Christy Carlson Romano.
