New Kids on the Block - Members, Ages, Trivia

They won Favorite Pop/Rock Band/Duo/Group at the American Music Awards in 1990. In total, they have sold more than 80 million albums worldwide. 

Trivia

Their second and third albums, Hangin' Tough and Step by Step, both reached #1 on the U.S. Billboard 200 chart. They disbanded between 1994 and 2007.
