Aleksi Koli - Age, Family, Bio

Finnish YouTube gaming personality who is widely known for his MrJallu101 channel's content. He has gained popularity there for his litany of gaming playthroughs, including for the FIFA and Call of Duty franchises.

Before Fame

He began his YouTube channel on June 9, 2011.

Trivia

He has amassed more than 210,000 subscribers to his YouTube channel. He has also earned more than 60,000 followers to his personal HerraJallu Instagram account.

Family Life

He was born and raised in Finland.

Associated With
