Edeoga Confirms He Is Now Enugu Labour Party Guber Candidate

The immediate past Commissioner of Environment in Enugu State, Hon. Chijioke Edeoga has announced he is now the governorship candidate for Enugu State Labour Party.

Naija News understands that there has been rumours of the politician dumping the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) after losing in the party’s primary election a month ago.

Speaking with Daily Post on Sunday, Edeoga said he is now the Labour Party governorship candidate in the State for the 2023 elections.

“Yes, it’s true. I’m now the Labour Party governorship candidate,” he told Daily Post.
