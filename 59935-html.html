GOODNEWS: Funke Akindele Seals Another Multi Million Naira Deal

Nollywood actress, Funke Akindele has signed a contract with a sport betting outfit, 1960BET.

* Funke

According to a source, the contract is said to worth millions of naira and it would see the actress work as the brand ambassador of the company. The actress, popularly called 'Jenifer' by her fans, would also be used for promotional activities of 1960 sport betting outfit.

Already, Funke has shot a commercial for the company. Few days ago, the company engaged Funke in a video shoot that took place around Jebba in Ebute Metta area of Lagos.

The commercial is expected to run on both national and international television stations.
