Paris Berelc - Age, Family, Bio

Gymnast who was once considered one of the premier young gymnasts in the United States. Also an actress, she played Molly in the 2015 TV movie Invisible Sister and starred as Skylar Storm on the Disney XD series Mighty Med and Lab Rats: Elite Force. In 2018, she began starring on Netflix's Alexa & Katie.

Before Fame

She started training for gymnastics at six years old. 

Trivia

In 2013, she put her gymnastics career on hold so that she could pursue acting, debuting on the show Mighty Med. 

Family Life

She was raised in the suburbs of Milwaukee, Wisconsin. She has three younger sisters named Skye, Bless and Joelie. She and Jack Griffo had an on-again, off-again relationship that began in 2018. Rhys Athayde later became her boyfriend.

Associated With

On Mighty Med, she starred along popular actor Bradley Steven Perry.
