Tega Flees Instagram Barely 24 Hrs After Crying Out

Former Big Brother Housemate, Tega Dominic has deactivated her Instagram account barely 24 hours after crying out over constant online bullying.

Naija News earlier reported that the 29-year-old housemate was dragged and chastised by many Nigerians for her alleged affair with fellow housemate, Boma.

Even though, the married housemate, who recently condemned those speaking about her actions in the house publicly, apologised for her mistakes and misgivings, Nigerians have continued to spite her, throwing hurtful words at her. 

She, however, via the microblogging platform on Tuesday, disclosed that she was not perfect but done with being constantly judged for a mistake she apologized for.

She also noted that she’s was mentally exhausted and done with online bullying. 

In her own words; “I am not perfect but I am done! Done with the negativity. Done with the online bullying. Done with being judged constantly. I am mentally exhausted”

A quick check on her Instagram by Naija News correspondence this morning shows the married former housemate has dumped and deactivated her Instagram page.
