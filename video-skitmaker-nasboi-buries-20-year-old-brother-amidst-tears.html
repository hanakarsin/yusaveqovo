Skitmaker Nasboi Buries 20-Year-Old Brother Amidst Tears

Nigerian skitmaker cum singer, Lawal Michael Nasiru, popularly known as Nasboi, has finally laid his only brother to rest.

Naija News reported that the skitmaker announced his brother’s demise in a post via his Instagram page on Saturday, 29 July.

Nasboi also shared an emotional photo of himself in tears.

According to him, the deceased, who is 20 years old, died on Friday night.

Reacting to the post, many Nigerian celebrities stormed his comment section to mourn with him and also console him.

Taking to his Instagram story on Sunday, Nasboi shared a video of how his brother was laid to rest.

The skitmaker was seen alongside sympathizers who were heard crying over the painful demise.

When I Will Stop Mourning Sound Sultan

In other news, legendary singer, Innocent Idibia, better known as 2Face Idibia, on Tuesday paid tribute to his late friend and colleague Sound Sultan.

It would be recalled that the singer died in July 2021 after battling Angioimmunoblastic T-Cell Lymphoma. He was aged 44.

Taking to his Instagram on the second death anniversary, 2Face shared old photos of himself and the deceased, vowing to mourn his death till he joins the late singer.

Last year, 2baba marked the first anniversary, stressing how badly he missed Sound Sultan.
