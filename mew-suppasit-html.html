Mew Suppasit - Age, Family, Bio

Television actor who is widely recognized for his work on the drama series TharnType. He has also appeared on Why R U? and What the Duck: The Series and its sequel What the Duck 2: Final Call.

Before Fame

He guest starred on a 2017 episode of I Am Your King.

Trivia

He obtained several degrees in industrial engineering.

Family Life

He was born Suppasit Jongcheveevat to a father named Boonsak and a mother named Suporn. He has a sister named Jomkwan.

Associated With

Both he and Gulf Kanawut were cast on TharnType.
