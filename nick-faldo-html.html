Nick Faldo - Age, Family, Bio

Hall of Fame PGA golfer who won three U.S. Open championships and three Masters.

Before Fame

He was inspired by Jack Nicklaus, and he borrowed a set of clubs from his neighbors after watching the Golden Bear on TV.

Trivia

He was ranked the No. 1 golfer in the world for nearly two years straight.

Family Life

He married three times and fathered four children: Natalie, Matthew, Georgia, and Emma Scarlett.

Associated With

His Faldo Series Academy saw many outstanding graduates leave its ranks, including Rory McIlroy.
