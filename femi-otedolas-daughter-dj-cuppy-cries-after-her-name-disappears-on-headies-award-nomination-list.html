Femi Otedola's Daughter, DJ Cuppy Cries After Her Name Disappears On Headies Award Nomination List

Nigerian billionaire daughter, Florence Ifeoluwa Otedola, better known as DJ Cuppy has expressed disappointment after her name was not listed in the Headies Awards.

Naija News understands that the organizers of the Headies on Friday released the list of nominees for the 14th edition of the music awards in recognition of outstanding achievements in the Nigerian music industry.

Davido, Omah Lay, Burna Boy, Falz, Fireboy DML, Mayorkun, DJ Neptune, Wizkid will battle for the Viewers Choice award while Burna Boy, Davido, Wizkid, Mayorkun, and Tiwa Savage will battle for the Artiste Of The Year award, Naija News reports.

However, the 28-year-old Nigerian female Disc Jockey on Friday, December 4 took to her micro-blogging site account to cry and express her disappointment for been snubbed in the award nominee list.
