'Cartel Crew' Latest News and Updates

The reality TV series Cartel Crew follows the lives of families previously connected to cartels in Miami.

The original cast featured Michael Blanco (the son of "Cocaine Godmother" Griselda Blanco), his wife, Marie Ramirez-D'Arellano, Kat "Tatu Baby" Flores, Stephanie Acevedo, Nicole Zavala, Dayana Castellanos, and Carlos "Loz" Oliveros.

Featured guests have included Mike "Majix" Yuen, Tatu Baby's ex Eddie Soto, and El Chapo's wife Emma Coronel Aispuro.
