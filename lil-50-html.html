Lil 50 - Trivia, Family, Bio

Before Fame

He began releasing music in early 2022. One of his first releases is the "START IT OFF FREESTYLE."

Trivia

His music videos have been viewed more than 22 million times on his official YouTube channel. He engages with fans on Instagram where he posts fashion and lifestyle photos.

Family Life

He was born in Cleveland, Ohio.

Associated With
