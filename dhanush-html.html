Dhanush - Age, Family, Bio

Tamil actor who won a 2012 Best Actor Award from the Filmfare Awards South for his role in Aadukalam. He also gained fame for his roles in 3, Polladhavan, and Yaaradi Nee Mohini.

Before Fame

He studied at the St. John's Matriculation Higher Secondary School Alwarthirunagar before beginning his acting career.

Trivia

His first film, Aadukalam, won six National Film Awards.

Family Life

He married Aishwarya Rajinikanth in 2004. The couple had two children. They announced their separation in January 2022 but later reconciled. 

Associated With

He worked with composer AR Rahman on the 2013 song "Kadal Raasa Naan."
