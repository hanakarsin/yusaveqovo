Katie Couric - Age, Family, Bio

NBC Today co-host who became a CBS news anchor. She also hosted programs on ABC, making her one of the few anchors who appear on the U.S. Big Three.

Before Fame

In 1979, she earned an English degree from the University of Virginia.

Trivia

She is a New York Times bestselling author.

Family Life

She married Jay Monahan on January 24, 1989 and they had two children together before he passed away in 1998. She then married financier John Molner on June 21, 2014.

Associated With

Her beloved sister Emily died of pancreatic cancer at 54; the same disease afflicted Patrick Swayze.
