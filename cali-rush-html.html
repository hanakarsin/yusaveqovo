Cali Rush - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube star and daughter of vlogger Tray Rush who appears on her father's popular channel TodayWithTray alongside her mother, Keshia Rush, and older brother. Together, they also star on their family channel, The Rush Fam. She has also had her own channel called Cali's Playhouse, which has gained her over 1.1 million subscribers. It has featured vlogs and stories from her life. 

Before Fame

In March 2014, she made her internet debut in the Tray Rush Instagram photo simply captioned "My motivation."

Trivia

She is the star of one of TodayWithTray's most popular videos, the 2016 vlog "WHAT'S WRONG WITH CALI??" which earned more than 5 million views within the year. She dressed up as a skeleton for Halloween in 2021 and posted a video in her costume on Instagram. 

Family Life

She and her older brother, Kameiro, share the YouTube channel FamousTubeKIDS.

Associated With

Like Laura of J House Vlogs, she is the child of popular YouTube family vloggers.
