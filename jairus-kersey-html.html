Jairus Kersey - Age, Family, Bio

Lead vocalist of the alternative rock band Alive Like Me who released a debut studio album titled Only Forever in 2014.

Before Fame

He and bassist David Knox formed the band in 2013 and put out a Craigslist ad to find a drummer and guitarist.

Trivia

He and Alive Like Me signed with Rise Records in January of 2014.

Family Life

He has a younger brother named Jaedan. In 2015, he began a relationship with pop singer Acacia Brinley, whom he later married in April 2018. He and Acacia have daughters named Brinley and Rosemary together. Their third child, a son, was born in October 2020.

Associated With

Alive Like Me joined Blessthefall as one of the numerous bands to play Warped Tour 2015.
