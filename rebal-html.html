Rebal D - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube comedian known for his Rebal D channel, which has accumulated more than 2.1 million subscribers through a combination of pranks, skits, and reaction videos. He is also known to occasionally stream on YouNow. He has also amassed 300,000 followers on his rebal_official Instagram account. The account has featured mostly modeling photographs.

Before Fame

Though his channel was created in 2011, the first video, "Lets Rant: LEARN TO APPRECIATE (Syrian Baby - Aylan Kurdi)," wasn't posted until September 2015.

Trivia

One of his most popular YouTube videos is the August 2016 video "PRANKING MY EX GIRLFRIEND WITH SONG LYRICS TEXTS (We Don't Talk Anymore)." He has often posted videos about TikTok trends and celebrities. 

Family Life

His sister is YouTuber Judy D. He has lived in Quito, Ecuador.

Associated With

In the July 2016 video "REMAKING POPULAR MUSICAL.LYS," he mimicked stars like Baby Ariel, Liza Koshy, and Cameron Dallas.
