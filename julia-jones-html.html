Julia Jones - Age, Family, Bio

Actress best known as Leah Clearwater in the third installment of the Twilight saga. Her other credits include Black Cloud and Jonah Hex. 

Before Fame

She began acting in commercials when she was eight years old. She attended Columbia University, where she studied English. 

Trivia

In 2015, she appeared in The Ridiculous 6 opposite Adam Sandler. 

Family Life

She was born in the Jamaica Plane district of Boston. 

Associated With

She was in the film Hell Ride, which was directed by Quentin Tarantino.
