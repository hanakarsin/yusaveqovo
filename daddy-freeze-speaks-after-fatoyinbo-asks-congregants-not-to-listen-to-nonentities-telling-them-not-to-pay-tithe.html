Daddy Freeze Speaks After Fatoyinbo Asks Congregants Not To Listen To Nonentities Telling Them Not T

Popular Nigerian OAP, Daddy Freeze has responded to Biodun Fatoyinbo preachings on paying tithes.

Recall that Naija News had earlier reported that Fatoyinbo asked his congregants not to listen to those who were asking them not to pay tithe because rich people like Elon Musk do not pay tithe.

He stated that a child of God has a covenant with God therefore the required ten percent must be given.

Reacting to Fatoyinbo claims,  Daddy Freeze asked the clergyman if he would be up for a debate based on scriptural backings.

Daddy Freeze is known for constantly questioning the teachings of clergymen on subjects such as tithes and divorce.

In other news, Media mogul and manager Tunji Balogun also known as Teebillz has praised Tacha as the only successful Bbnaija housemate that has not opened her legs.
