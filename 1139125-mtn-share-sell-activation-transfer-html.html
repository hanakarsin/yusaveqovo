MTN Share and Sell activation and transfer guide in 2024

MTN is a leading mobile network operator in Africa. It offers the benefits of modern connected life with millions of Nigerians. If you are a subscriber, you should learn how to activate MTN share and sell. Having this information will help you share airtime with those you love.

Have you been wondering how to activate MTN share and sell? MTN Share, also known as Share ‘N’ Sell, is a service that allows you to transfer airtime from one MTN user to another without charge.

MTN Share and Sell activation and transfer guide

Have you been wondering how to share airtime on MTN? If so, do not fret because this guide will elaborate on how to do this without strain.

How to register Share and Sell on MTN

Learning how to activate Share and Sell on MTN is important if you want to share airtime on this mobile network. Before using this service, you must be a new and existing prepaid or postpaid customer on this network.

This means you should have a registered MTN SIM card. It must also be inserted into your mobile phone device. Follow the steps below to discover how to register for MTN Share and Sell.

  • The primary requirement for Share and Sell on MTN is to change the default PIN, which is 0000.
  • To do this, send an SMS with your Default PIN, new PIN and new PIN to 777, e.g. send an SMS with ‘0000 1234 1234’ to 777.
  • Alternatively, you can change it by dialling *777*Default PIN*New PIN* New PIN# on your phone. Next, click on send/press OK, e.g. dial *777*0000*1234*1234#, then send OK.
  • You will receive a confirmation SMS informing you that you successfully changed your PIN. You can now transfer airtime to your loved ones using SMS or USSD.

MTN Share and Sell using SMS

Follow the simple steps below to transfer airtime to your phone.

  • Send an SMS with the word transfer, followed by the recipient’s number, amount and PIN to 777, e.g. Transfer 08031234567 1500 1234 to 777.
  • You will receive an SMS asking you to confirm that you want to make the transfer.
  • Confirm the transaction by sending an SMS with the word YES to 777.

Using MTN Share and Sell code

If you do not want to use the SMS option above, you can use a short code to transfer airtime.

  • On your mobile phone, dial *777*recipient’s number*amount*PIN#, then click on send or press OK, e.g. *777*08031234567*1500*1234# and then send OK.
  • Once the transfer goes through, you will receive an SMS confirming a successful transfer.

Points you should note

You should have the following information at the back of your head when learning how to migrate to MTN Share and Sell.

  • Sharing airtime using this service does not attract charges.
  • You will only be able to transfer airtime in Naira and not kobo amounts, i.e. you can transfer ₦71.00 but not ₦71.99.
  • The minimum limit for a single transaction is ₦50.00, while the maximum is ₦5,000.00.
  • The maximum you can transfer in one day to single or multiple accounts is ₦100,000.00.
  • There is no limit to the maximum number of transactions you can make in one day.
  • If your prepaid account lacks enough airtime to cover the amount you have requested to transfer, you will receive an error message. The transaction will not be completed.
  • If you are a postpaid client, you will only be able to transfer airtime when your account is in positive, i.e. if your account balance is ₦-300.00, you will be unable to use this service.
