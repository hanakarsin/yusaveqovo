Carolina Samani - Age, Family, Bio

Social media celebrity and fitness enthusiast who found her biggest fan base on Instagram. Her popular modeling, swimsuit and lifestyle photos have helped her pick up more than 250,000 total followers.

Before Fame

In November 2015, she uploaded her first Instagram pic which was an image of a colorful sky.

Trivia

She authored the digital e-books Caro Meals and No Pain, No Gain or Stay the Same.

Family Life

Her hometown is Charlotte, North Carolina but she moved to Jacksonville, Florida. She dated Taylor Caniff in 2019.

Associated With

Ashley Dee and Maria Del Mar are two other Instagram stars who became known for their swimsuit pictures.
