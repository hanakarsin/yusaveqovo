Kaelyn Pannier - Age, Family, Bio

Popular YouTuber with a self-titled channel who posts morning routine videos, DIY projects, and other content relating to fashion, beauty and lifestyle. She also began posting numerous vlogs with her boyfriend on her Kaelyn & Jack channel. 

Before Fame

She competed in a Miss Florida Junior Pre-Teen competition in 2011. 

Trivia

Her YouTube channel, which was launched in December of 2013, has earned over 160,000 subscribers. She has also sold her own custom apparel and accessories on Etsy. 

Family Life

She is from Florida. She has featured her mother and her sister Kendall on her YouTube channel. 

Associated With

She collaborated with Rachel Levin on a "Prank Calls" video.
