Yara Martinez - Age, Family, Bio

Cuban American actress known for her recurring roles on the Amazon political comedy series Alpha House, the CW's Jane the Virgin, and HBO's True Detective (season 2). In 2016, she began starring on CBS's Bull as Izzy. 

Before Fame

One of her earliest roles was on It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia, in the episode "Dennis and Dee Get A New Dad." 

Trivia

Her first role as a series regular was as Kelly on the prime time soap opera Hollywood Heights.

Family Life

She was born in Puerto Rico, and raised in Miami.

Associated With

Her role as Adriana De Portago on Alpha House has her butting heads from time to time with Senator Gil John Biggs, played by legendary actor John Goodman.
