BBNaija Erica 'Disgraced' By Fans - 'You Can Swallow Sperm On National TV

Swallowing sperms on national television has been listed as parts of former Big Brother Naija housemate, Erica’s talents.

This was coming after the reality show star took the microblogging site to list her talents following comments by her fans that they had no idea what she can do.

To clear their doubt on her endowered she is with talents, Erica took to her social media account, Twitter precisely to disclosed that she can sing, model, act, paint, install frontals and whine her waist.

She tweeted: “Lol since y’all are complaining I didn’t list my talents let me list them out
I can sing, model, act, paint, install frontals, whine my waist”.

However, social media users dragged Erica on the bird app after she listed the above. According to the netizens, she failed to add one talent she is very good at doing.

“She can swallow sperms on national television,” a social media user identified as Uyi on the bird app tweeted.
