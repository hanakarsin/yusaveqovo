Peter Haskell - Trivia, Family, Bio

Television actor remembered for his guest roles on such popular series as The Mary Tyler Moore Show, Charlie's Angels, Ryan's Hope, Frasier, and JAG.

Before Fame

After briefly serving in the United States Army, he earned an undergraduate degree from Harvard University.

Trivia

He appeared in the second and third installments of the Child's Play horror films.

Family Life

His marriage to Annie Compton lasted for over a decade. In 1974, he divorced Compton and married Dianne Tolmich.

Associated With

Early in his career, he appeared with acclaimed actor James Earl Jones in an Off-Broadway production of a play called The Love Nest.
