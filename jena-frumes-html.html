Jena Frumes - Age, Family, Bio

Model best known for her popular Instagram account, which has attracted over 4.7 million followers. She has been featured in several forums and sites across the web. Her self-titled Youtube channel has had multiple life updates, vlogs and challenges posted on it. 

Before Fame

She attended North Carolina Central University in Durham, NC.

Trivia

In 2017, she began appearing on the improv show Wild 'N Out. She has created her own collection of sportswear called Intensity by Jena Frumes. 

Family Life

She was born and raised in New Jersey and would later relocate to Los Angeles, CA. She started dating singer Jason Derulo in 2020. Their son, Jason King Derulo, was born May 8, 2021. In September Derulo and Frumes ended their relationship. 

Associated With

She featured Lala Milan in a YouTube video titled "How To Install Lace Wig" in June 2019.
