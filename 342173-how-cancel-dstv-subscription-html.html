How to cancel DStv subscription

DStv is one of the leading satellite TV service providers in Africa. Since its inception, the company has expanded to over 40 African nations. Despite its popularity and unique packages, sometimes, you might opt to exit the service. Whether you are switching to another satellite TV provider to lower your monthly costs or you don’t need their packages anymore, you can easily cancel your DStv subscription.

The procedure to cancelling DStv is simple and straightforward. You do not need any technical skills, aptitudes, or knacks to nail it. Below is a simple guide on how to suspend DStv subscription through various methods.

Can I suspend my DStv subscription?

Yes, you can. If you no longer need to access their packages, there are quite a few methods you can use to terminate your account. Note that this is a cancellation and not upgrading or downgrading from one boutique to another.

    How to cancel DStv debit order

    A debit order is a payment method that gives consent to collect your monthly subscription from your bank account. It is an agreement between you and DStv hence your bank can never cancel it.

    To cancel this, you need to fill in a DStv cancellation form or revoke instructions via writing at any of their branches a month before your next billing cycle. It will give the management an ample time to work on your request within the last month you need to enjoy the services.

    Does DStv refund after cancellation?

    Assuming that you had paid for a particular month then you cancel the subscription, will you be reimbursed? No, you can never be refunded the amount paid as the company operates on a prepay basis. It means that you first pay to access your specific package channels.

    Hence, you will continue enjoying all the DStv TV networks in your boutique until the last date of your billing cycle. Cancellation will automatically take place before the next payment exact date and time.

    What happens if I don’t pay my DStv?

    MultiChoice services are prepaid. You should make payments first before accessing any channels. If you fail to pay for the subscription on your due date, then this might result in a disconnection.
