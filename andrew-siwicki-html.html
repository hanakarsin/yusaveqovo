Andrew Siwicki - Age, Family, Bio

Internet content creator who had found success on Vine before earning more followings on Snapchat and Instagram. He is well known for his collaborations with fellow internet personalities Garrett Watts and Shane Dawson. He is also a freelance music producer. 

Before Fame

He worked as an intern for Lily's Talent Agency, Demi & Cooper Advertising and William Morris Endeavor. 

Trivia

He was a member of the 2016 graduating class of Northern Illinois University. He was the student manager for the men's and women's golf program, as well as the men's basketball team at the school. 

Family Life

He is from the Chicago, Illinois area. 

Associated With

He is a friend of Ricky Montgomery, and helped him put together something called Ricky Montgomery's Vlog.
