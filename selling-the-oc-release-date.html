Netflix Confirmed the Summer Premiere Date for 'Selling the O.C.'

'Selling the O.C.' is Netflix's next real estate reality series, and its a spin-off of 'Selling Sunset.' When is the release date? It's sooner than fans might expect.

Since it debuted in 2019, Selling Sunset has been a top hit in the reality TV genre for Netflix. The real estate series features gorgeous, high-end California listings, and plenty of drama among the agents at The Oppenheim Group.

It's no surprise, then, that Netflix looked to expand the Selling Sunset empire with a few direct spin-off shows, and with other real estate-centric programs.

Selling Tampa debuted on the streamer in December 2021, and Netflix confirmed that the second Selling Sunset spin-off, Selling the O.C., was in the works that same year.

A trailer for the latter series aired during the Selling Sunset Season 5 reunion, and it revealed which Oppenheim Group agents in the Newport Beach office would be in the cast. Now, two months after the reunion dropped, Netflix has officially revealed the release date for Selling the O.C. Read on to find out everything you need to know about the upcoming show.

When is the release date for 'Selling the O.C.' Season 1?

The entire first season of the Orange County-based series will drop on Netflix on Wednesday, Aug. 24, 2022.

In addition to confirming the Selling the O.C. release date, Netflix also renewed Selling Sunset for another two seasons. That means that the real estate series will continue at least through Season 7.

The 'Selling the O.C.' cast features agents from The Oppenheim Group office in Newport Beach.

The streaming giant has also shared the names of The Oppenheim Group agents who are in the cast.

While Selling Sunset follows the female agents at the L.A. office, Selling the O.C. will feature a mix of male and female stars.

The show, which largely filmed at the office in the Newport Beach area of Orange County, will feature brokers Jason and Brett Oppenheim. At this time, it appears as if the twins will be the only Selling Sunset stars to appear on the spin-off.

The cast list also includes Alexandra "Alex" Hall, Alexandra Jarvis, Alexandra Rose, Austin Victoria, Brandi Marshall, Gio Helou, Kayla Cardona, Lauren Brito, Polly Brindle, Sean Palmieri, and Tyler Stanaland.

Like Selling Sunset stars Vanessa Villela and Chrishell Stause, Austin Victoria has also worked as an actor in addition to being a real estate agent.

Tyler Stanaland also has similar backstory to Chrishell's (on the early seasons) — he's married to an actor. The agent married Pitch Perfect actress Brittany Snow in 2020.

While Chrishell and This Is Us actor Justin Hartley split up in 2019, Tyler and Brittany are still going strong.

Though many of the Selling the OC cast members are engaged or married to non-agents, co-stars Alex Hall and Sean Palmieri are currently in a relationship. This could potentially provide some drama on the series.

Until Selling the OC premieres, you can stream all five seasons of Selling Sunset (and the reunion!) on Netflix.
