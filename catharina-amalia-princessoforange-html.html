Catharina-Amalia, Princess of Orange - Age, Family, Bio

First in line to the Dutch throne, this royal was born Catharina-Amalia Beatrix Carmen Victoria. Shortly after her birth, she assumed the title of Princess of Orange. She is the oldest child of Willem-Alexander of the Netherlands and Queen Máxima.

Before Fame

Her birth was celebrated by numerous cannon salutes throughout the Kingdom of the Netherlands.

Trivia

Her mother, Queen Maxima, grew up in Argentina as the child of politician Jorge Zorreguieta. In the Summer of 2022, she attended the 18th birthday celebration of Princess Ingrid Alexandra of Norway.

Family Life

She and her younger siblings, Princesses Ariane and Alexia, were born in The Hague, Netherlands, to Queen Maxima and King Willem-Alexander.

Associated With

She and Queen Juliana both belong to the Dutch royal family.
