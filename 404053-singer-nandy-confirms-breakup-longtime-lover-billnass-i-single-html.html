Celebrated Tanzanian singer Nandy has for the first time confirmed to the world that she is single.

In a recent interview, the musician disclosed that she is no longer with her longtime lover, rapper Billnass.

Speaking during an interview with Close FM, Nandy was hesitant to let the cat out of the bag but finally did.

According to the musician, she is currently on a break with her man but did not reveal why they decided to.
