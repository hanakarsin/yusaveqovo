Nanyuki woman with BCom degree withdraws children from school, terms education satanic

- Esther Njeri was arrested following an operation to trace school-going children who were still being held at home

- Njeri's two children had not reported to school since school resumed in-person learning in early January 2021

- Ironically the 36-year-old woman holds a Bachelor's Degree in Commerce and another in Accounting

Police officers in Nanyuki town have detained a woman identified as Esther Njeri who is reported to have pulled her children out of school claiming that education is satanic.

Two children were pulled out of school by the 36-year-old mother; a 13-year-old girl in Form Two and an 8-year-old boy in elementary school, Citizen TV reported.

The area chief discovered that Esther’s children had not reported back to school following an operation to trace school-going children who were still being held at home.

According to the chief who was flanked by village elders during the operation, the two children had not reported to school since school resumed in-person learning in early January 2021.

Njeri, ironically, holds a Bachelor's Degree in Commerce and another in Accounting.

She recently began warning friends and family that the end of the world was near, asking them to repent before the end of the year, her confused neighbours said.

She also said that comparing her tribulations to the biblical Shadrack Meshack and Abednego, she is ready to face the law.

Education Principal Secretary Belio Kipsang issued a circular earlier in the year ordering security officials to trace children being held at home after the re-opening of schools.

This followed reports of students not reporting to school.
