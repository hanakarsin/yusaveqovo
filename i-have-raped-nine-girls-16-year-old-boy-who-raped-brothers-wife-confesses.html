'I Have Raped Nine Girls'

The Western Nigeria Security Network codenamed, Amotekun, has arrested a 16-year-old teenager, identified as Precious, for allegedly raping his brother’s wife and nine girls in Ondo State.

Naija News reports the suspect was paraded before newsmen on Friday, November 11, 2022, and he admitted to committing the crime.

Speaking with journalists, the state commander of Amotekun, Adetunji Adeleye, said that the suspect raped the wife of his brother when he travelled outside the town.

In an interview, Precious confessed to having raped nine other girls at Laosun who are between the ages of 19 and 25.

The suspect said he walked into the house and met his brother’s wife sleeping naked and he took advantage of her.

He noted that he had once been arrested for stealing 25 litres of palm oil, caught during a crime, and beaten.

In his word: “In Laosun, I have raped nine girls. Some of them are 19 or 25 years, and at night, I will go into their rooms and start sleeping with them. I do not use guns or knives to threaten them. Sometimes I would be caught and they would beat me and leave me.

“I have been arrested before. I was arrested for stealing 25 litres of palm oil. I wanted to gather money to use to buy a phone. I came to the house and saw my brother’s wife sleeping naked. I went into the room and slept with her.”

The Amotekun Commander said the suspect would soon be charged to court.
