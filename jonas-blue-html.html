Jonas Blue - Age, Family, Bio

Singer and music producer primarily focused in the dance-pop genre who became known for his hit single "Fast Car." He later released a second single called "Perfect Strangers."

Before Fame

He was born and raised in London, England.

Trivia

"Fast Car" reached #1 on iTunes in Germany, Sweden, Australia, and New Zealand.

Family Life

His real name is Guy James Robin. He has dated model Melinda London.

Associated With

His single "Fast Car" peaked at #2 just behind Zayn Malik's "Pillowtalk" on the UK Singles Chart.
