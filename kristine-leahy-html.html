Kristine Leahy - Age, Family, Bio

TV host and sportscaster known for hosting American Ninja Warrior. She also joined Colin Cowherd on his radio show, The Herd, when he moved to Fox Sports One. She previously worked for CBS Sports Network and covered sports in Los Angeles.

Before Fame

She attended Boston University, where she founded a sports talk show for the school's television station. After graduating, she got a job at WEEI in Boston.

Trivia

She called a horse race in 2013, in the process becoming the first woman in California history to do so.

Family Life

She and her two younger brother grew up in Chicago during the height of the Michael Jordan era.

Associated With

She replaced Jenn Brown when she became the host of American Ninja Warrior.
