Domino Kirke - Age, Family, Bio

English singer-songwriter who co-founded the indie pop band, DOMINO. They've toured with the likes of Lily Allen and Gang of Four. She's also had solo success with her debut EP, The Guard. She's also worked as a doula and co-founded her own business.

Before Fame

She was musically trained by her father Simon Kirke, drummer for the bands Free and Bad Company.

Trivia

She's the sister of HBO's Girls star Jemima Kirke and Mozart in the Jungle's Lola Kirke.

Family Life

She married former Gossip Girl star Penn Badgley in a private ceremony in 2017. Their first child together, a son named James, was born in August 2020. She has a son named Cassius from a previous relationship.

Associated With

Her band DOMINO was featured in Lena Dunham's breakout film Tiny Furniture.
