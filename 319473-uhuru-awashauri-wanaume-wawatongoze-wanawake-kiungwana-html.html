Uhuru awashauri wanaume wawatongoze wanawake kiungwana

Rais Uhuru Kenyatta amewahimiza wanaume watafute mbinu kabambe ya kuwatongoza wanawake na kabla wafanya hivyo, lazima wawape heshima zao

Uhuru alisema binadamu waliumbwa sawa ila wanawake ni viumbe dhaifu ambao wanapaswa kulindwa ipasavyo.

"Wazee waheshimu akina mama sio kwenda kukosea mwanamke heshima. Wewe enda umtongoze pole pole ukibahatika sawa," Uhuru alisema.
