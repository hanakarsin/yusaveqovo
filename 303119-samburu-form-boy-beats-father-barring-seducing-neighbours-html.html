Samburu: Form Two boy beats up father for barring him from seducing neighbour's daughter

- The boy was angered by his father's intervention to stop him from making romantic advancements to a neghbour's daughter

- The Form Two student beat the old man up before running away

- Prior to that, the boy had allegedly stabbed and killed a man alleged to be the girl's dad

- Area chief Sammy Lothuru confirmed the incident

A Form Two student at Baragoi High School in Samburu county pounced on his father after he allegedly warned him against seducing a neighbour's teenage daughter.

It is alleged, prior to attacking his father, the enraged boy had stabbed and killed another man reported to be the girlfriend's father.

According to Citizen, the suspect who is on the run, was angered by his father's intervention to bar him from making romantic advancements to the girl.

Area chief Sammy Lothuru said the girl had rejected the boy's advancements and reported the matter to her father who proceeded to talk to the boy's dad asking him to stop his son from bothering his teenage daughter.

"The suspect was seducing his neighbour’s daughter, who seemingly rejected his advances and reported him to her father. The girl’s father approached the boy’s father to ask him to speak to his son. When the suspect’s father warned him against pursuing the girl. The errant boy, angered by his dad’s intervention, beat him man up and disappeared from home,” said Lothuru.

Police have since launched a manhunt on the 17-year-old boy
