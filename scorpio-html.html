Scorpios | Famous Birthdays

The eighth astrological sign, Scorpio is typically represented by the scorpion, but has also been associated with snakes and eagles. Scorpio is a water sign alongside Pisces and Cancer. 

Trivia

Musician Adam Ant released a song in 1985 called "Scorpio Rising" which in reference to the sign of Scorpio. The sign's name has also inspired numerous characters in popular movies and TV shows such as the Scorpio Killer in the 1971 film Dirty Harry and the title character in the 1973 spy film Scorpio.
