Michelle Rodriguez (Movie Actress) - Age, Family, Bio

Actress best known for her action roles in The Fast and the Furious and TV's Lost. She received critical acclaim for her role in the 2000 film Girlfight. She played Rain Ocampo in Resident Evil and Resident Evil: Retribution. 

Before Fame

She was expelled from five schools before pursuing acting. Girlfight was her first film. 

Trivia

She starred in James Cameron's film Avatar, which became one of the most successful movies of all time. She voiced Rosie Velasco in the video game True Crime: Streets of LA. 

Family Life

She dated Vin Diesel in 2001. She also briefly dated Lenny Kravitz and Cara Delevingne. She has two brothers, Omar and Raul. 

Associated With

She was in The Fast and the Furious with Paul Walker.
