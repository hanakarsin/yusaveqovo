Maia Reficco - Age, Family, Bio

Actress and singer who rose to fame for her portrayal of Kally on the hit Nickelodeon musical telenovela Kally's MashUp. In 2019, she began starring as the title character in the Broadway production Evita. She released her first single, "Tuya," in September 2020. 

Before Fame

She made her professional acting debut in 2017. 

Trivia

In 2017, she attended the Kids' Choice Awards Argentina event where she was nominated for Best Actress. That same year, she won for Favorite TV Actress at the Meus Prêmios Nick. 

Family Life

She is originally from Boston, Massachusetts but moved to Buenos Aires, Argentina when she was younger. 

Associated With

Both she and Miranda Cosgrove appeared on popular Nickelodeon programs.
