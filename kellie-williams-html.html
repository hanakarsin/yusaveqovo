Kellie Shanygne Williams - Age, Family, Bio

American actress best known for her role as Laura Winslow, the object of Steve Urkel's affections on the hit ABC sitcom Family Matters, which ran from 1989 to 1998.

Before Fame

A shy child, she did not talk much, leading her father to enroll her in theater classes to break her out of her shell.

Trivia

After ending her run with Family Matters, she made guest appearances on TV shows What About Joan and The Parkers, before coming to host the reality series Clean House.

Family Life

She married Hannibal Jackson in 2009. They had two children, John and Hannah. 

Associated With

She and her co-star Jaleel White, who played Urkel, were good friends.
