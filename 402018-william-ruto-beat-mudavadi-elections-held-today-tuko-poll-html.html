William Ruto would beat Mudavadi if elections were held today, TUKO poll

- Most of the respondents felt Deputy President William Ruto was more aggressive and charismatic than Musalia Mudavadi and would most likely attract more votes

- Some noted whereas Mudavadi would make a better leader than Ruto, he would need a lot of support from other political bigwigs in order to match the DP's political muscle

- On Twitter, 72.1% of the respondents voted for Ruto as the most formidable presidential candidate while 15% voted for ANC Leader Musalia Mudavadi

Deputy President William Ruto and Amani National Congress (ANC) Party Leader Musalia Mudavadi are among a few Kenyans who have so far declared interest in succeeding President Uhuru Kenyatta in 2022.

The two have emerged as potential frontrunners in the race alongside Wiper Democratic Movement (WDM) Party Leader Kalonzo Musyoka and Opposition chief Raila Odinga who is yet to make a declaration.

TUKO.co.ke sought the opinion of Kenyans on who between Ruto and Mudavadi would carry the day if presidential elections were held today.

In a question that was posted on all our social media platforms, majority of Kenyans rallied behind the DP as the most formidable presidential candidate so far.

While some said Mudavadi would make a better president than Ruto, they ruled out his ability to floor the second in command on account of his tenderness and gentleman's politics.

The respondents, in the comment section, said the ANC leader would need a lot of support from top political leaders of the calibre of the president and Opposition leader in order to match Ruto's political muscle.

Simon Onaya Mike:

"The fact is Ruto will carry the day but Musalia is the right choice, the guy is very sober...William and Sonko belong to the same WhatsApp group...they are very popular but they don't deliver. My humble opinion though".

Aggrey Mallo Muhando:

"Mudavadi's humble nature will cost him,he is very mature and sober but Kenyans can't see that we love dramas and political comedies which Mudavadi lucks, but do I say....?

Kenboi Cindy:

"Comparing Rutto with Mudavadi is just like comparing Manchester city with Arsenal. Replace him with Raila and we'll decide appropriately".

Stanley Muhia:

"Dr William all the way Mudavadi will always support Raila".

Kaplelach Gideon:

"William Samoei Ruto. He has the energy, the spirit, the vision and the charisma to lead this country".

Bernard Kapkatet:

"Ruto 95% but if he was competing with Raila things would have been change.50 by 50".

Geoffrey Mweu:

"Many will vote for Ruto but the best leader for this country is Musalia Mudavad but the problem with Kenyans they don't like the good people".

On Twitter, 72.1% voted for Ruto as the potential successor of Uhuru in 2022 while 15% voted for Mudavadi as the man likely to win the State House race. 9.2% said they would not vote while 3.6% were undecided.

Mudavadi has emerged as one of Uhuru's preferred successors with reports indicating the two have been holding secret meetings through their proxies to negotiate and plan for 2022.

Uhuru is also said to be keen on wooing Raila and his bastions to support the ANC leader in order to form a formidable team that would edge out the DP.

Kalonzo, KANU leader Gideon Moi and Moses Wetangu'la of FORD Kenya have been working closely with Mudavadi in the recent past and are likely to support him should he get the president's endorsement.

Ruto fell out of favour in the succession matrix after disagreeing with the president and is currently popularising his bid using the hustler narrative.
