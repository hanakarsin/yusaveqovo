How to buy Safaricom unlimited bundles

In this modern era, telecommunication operators understand that most people require budget-friendly internet deals for work and entertainment. It makes a lot of sense to see the telecommunication operators trying to outdo or outshine each other with pro-data services and products. Safaricom has introduced a good deal that will definitely change the game for Kenyans in need of fast internet services. The deal is under their new tunukiwa packages. It has a good snick for the 4G internet users that always want to stay connected.

Here, we will share with you the Safaricom unlimited bundles offer that is tucked well on their ‘tunukiwa’ option, how to purchase it, and other ‘tunukiwa’ deals.

How to purchase Safaricom 4G unlimited internet

Unlimited 4G bundles valid for 24 hours sounds good. Right? You work or get entertained online without worries of your data running out. The newly introduced unlimited 4G data for Safaricom internet Kenya that goes for Ksh. 300 and is valid for 24 hours is a very impressive deal.

Here is how to access the bundle options and buy them.

1. Dial *544# or *444# and press the call button.

2. Select the option 'Buy tunukiwa bundles'.

3. Choose the bundle option number and select 'send'.

4. Select whether you want to pay via airtime or M-pesa then click send.

5. A confirmation message will pop-up. Select 'accept' and proceed.

Purchasing an internet bundle is that simple. You can also buy the bundles directly from the mysafaricom app that is downloadable from playstore. Here is the process.

1. Log in into our account on mySafaricom app.

2. Click on 'Tunikiwa'.

3. Select the bundle you would like to purchase and click the 'buy' button.

4. A prompt will appear. Click 'Confirm' to proceed.

Note: Ensure you have sufficient airtime for the bundle you are purchasing.

You can also purchase the Safaricom unlimited internet that goes for sh. 100 4G which is valid for 3 hours and so many other options.
