The decomposing body of Cohen was discovered inside an underground water tank at his Kitusuru residence on Friday, September 13, 2019.

In a touching tribute published in the Daily Nation on Tuesday, July 20, in his memory, his family said he was dearly missed and loved beyond measure.

"Although we remain grief-stricken, we are comforted by the many wonderful memories that we shared and with the knowledge that your spirit and legacy continue to shine on," read the tribute in part.

The tribute, however, did not recognise Cohen's wife, Sarah Wairimu who is the prime suspect in the murder of the businessman.

Tortured before death

The Directorate of Criminal Investigations boss George Kinoti confirmed the said water tank had been completely sealed with concrete and covered with bushy wood cuttings.

Cohen's body, which was wrapped in many layers of clothing revealed he was tortured.
