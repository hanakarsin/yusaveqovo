Shehu Sani Speaks On Murder Of Senator NaAllahs Son

The former lawmaker representing Kaduna Central senatorial district, Shehu Sani, has reacted to the killing of the eldest son of Senator Bala Na’Allah, Abdulkarim Bala Na’Allah on Sunday.

Naija News had earlier reported that unknown assailants killed the lawmaker’s son at his Malali home in Kaduna.

The Special Adviser to the lawmaker, Garba Mohammed while confirming the incident said the gunmen entered the house through the ceiling.

Mohammed further disclosed that the security guard of the deceased’s neighbour noticed that the gate was opened and raised the alarm, which led to the discovery of the remains of the pilot.

Sani in a reaction to the incident on Twitter described the death of Captain Abdulkarim Bala as an unfortunate one.

He also prayed that Allah forgives his shortcomings and accepts his peaceful soul.

The former lawmaker wrote, “The news of the killing of Captain Abdulkarim Bala Ibn Na’Allah the son of Senator Bala Na’Allah in Malali GRA Kaduna is tragic and unfortunate.

“It used to be the outskirts of our city that was unsafe and now it has gotten into the city. May Allah forgive his soul.”
