Kenny Braasch - Age, Family, Bio

Model who has been represented by Soul Artist Management. He was the focus of a VICTOR Magazine Men photoshoot in October 2017. He has over 300,000 followers on his kennybraasch10 Instagram account. He was on The Bachelorette season 16 and the seventh season of Bachelor in Paradise.

Before Fame

He competed in the 2014 NPC Junior National Championships.

Trivia

He appeared in the YouTube video "LIVING with Ashley Kitchen Originals" in October 2017 where he discussed how to eat to maintain model-like abs. 

Family Life

His sister is Kimberly Braasch. He got engaged to Mari Pepin-Solis on the season finale of Bachelor in Paradise.

Associated With

He and Abigail Heringer were both on season 7 of Bachelor in Paradise.
