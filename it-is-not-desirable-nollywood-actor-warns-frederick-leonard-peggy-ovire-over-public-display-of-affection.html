'It Is Not Desirable' - Nollywood Actor Warns Frederick Leonard, Peggy Ovire Over Public Display Of

Nollywood actor, Osagie Elegbe, has warned his colleagues Frederick Leonard and his wife, Peggy Ovire, over flaunting their love life online.

Naija News reports that Osagie issued the warning after Peggy took to her Instagram page to share a loved-up video with her husband inside a car.

According to the newly married, Frederick always makes her late for every appointment as he would not want her to leave his sight.

She wrote: “Every dammm time I’m stepping out, the appointment of 10 am this man delays me till 1 pm”

Reacting to the public display of affection, Osagie Elegbe, berated the couple, stating that showing their love life online is not desirable.

According to him, there are many people waiting to destroy happy marriages and the new couple should learn from people who have suffered the negative effect of social media on their union.

He wrote: “Another error showing how much you love each other on social media is not desirable, there are others waiting to tear it apart. Learn from others who are already eating their bitter pie”

Timi Dakolo Discloses Why Some People Do Not Reciprocate Love

Meanwhile, Nigerian singer and songwriter, Timi Dakolo, has revealed why some people do not reciprocate the love shown to them.

The father of two in a post shared on Twitter opined that some people are ignorant of the love they receive from others.

According to the ‘Iyawo mi’ crooner, such kind of people aren’t selfish or anything related to it but they grew up in homes where survival was a priority not love.

Timi Dakolo noted that such people want to survive by every means and the popular saying ‘to love and be loved’ is not top of their list.
