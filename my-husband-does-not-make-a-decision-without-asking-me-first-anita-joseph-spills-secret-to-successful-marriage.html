My Husband Does Not Make A Decision Without Asking Me First

Controversial Nollywood actress, Anita Joseph has shared her secret to a happy home.

The Anambra State-born thespian, while speaking via Instagram, explained how her husband, Michael Olagunju aka MC Fish treats her.

She revealed that her husband does not make a decision without asking her first, noting that he seeks her opinion in everything.

Anita explained that she also reciprocates the action and seeks his permission and opinion on issues.

The mother of one opined that adopting that kind of mindset is one of the secret to a happy home.

She maintained that sharing a spiritual connection with one’s spouse is very paramount in marriage.

The thespian then asked single ladies praying for husbands if they are ready to make such sacrifices towards a happy home.

She wrote, “He was my boyfriend first then he became my husband. My husband never makes any decision without asking me first. He needs my opinion in everything and trust me it works perfectly. I do same, I can’t do anything without telling my hubby. These are one of the lil secrets to a successful marriage. Spiritual connection between you and your husband is very important. For my sisters praying for a husband have you prepared yourself. Yours will be easy in Jesus name. Shallom Shallom Shallom.”
