Mizormac - Age, Family, Bio

Member of the UK drill crew Harlem Spartans who earned the reputation of the best younger drill rappers out of the UK. Some of his top tracks with the group included "Call Me A Spartan," "Kennington Where It Started" and "Grip & Ride."  

Before Fame

He is a childhood friend of fellow Harlem Spartans Blanco Harlem, Bis Harlem, TG Millian, Zico and Active. They broke though with the single "Kennington Bop." 

Trivia

He was a solo artist before joining Harlem Spartans. 

Family Life

He grew up in Kennington, South East London.

Associated With

He is also joined in Harlem Spartans by Loski.
