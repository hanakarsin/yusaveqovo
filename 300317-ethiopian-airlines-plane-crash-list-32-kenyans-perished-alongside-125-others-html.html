Ethiopian Airlines plane crash: List of all 32 Kenyans who perished alongside 125 others

- A number of different nationals were aboard the ill-fated Boeing 737 Max plane

- Among them, 32 Kenyans were confirmed dead from the crash

- An official list of names of all Kenyans in the flight has since been released

The names of the Kenyans who were on board the ill-fated Ethiopian Airlines flight en route to Nairobi has finally been released, TUKO.co.ke has learnt.

Even as the world is still coming to terms with the tragic aircraft accident, Kenyan has been the hardest hit as 32 locals were on board the aircraft.

Ethiopia airlines has since announced the commencement of forensic investigations to establish what exactly caused the crash which, according to reports, happened six minutes after takeoff.

An official statement released on Sunday, March 10 evening revealed stated that a number of Ethiopian stakeholders including Ethiopian Airlines, Ethiopian Civil Aviation and Ethiopian Transport Authority have already teamed up to commence investigations.

Meanwhile, Ethiopia's Prime Minister Abiy Ahmed has since issued a televised statement on behalf of the government where it was announced that Monday, March 11 would be set aside as a day of national mourning.

Notably, two bosses from the Kenyan Airports Authority (KAA) namely Otieno Juliet Murundu and Kabugi George Gikonyo were on board the plane.
