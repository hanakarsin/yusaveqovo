Lorena Andrea - Age, Family, Bio

British actress known for her starring role as Sister Lilith in Warrior Nun. She made her feature film debut in 2017's Papillon. In 2018, she starred in the comedy drama film Jesters.

Before Fame

She studied acting at the Anna Scher Theatre in Islington, Greater London. She was a competitive swimmer growing up. 

Trivia

She speaks both English and Spanish fluently. She has earned representation from Core MGMT. She has worked in television, film and theatre. 

Family Life

She was born to parents of Spanish and Colombian descent. She is fluent in Spanish.

Associated With

She and Alba Baptista worked together on the cast of Warrior Nun.
