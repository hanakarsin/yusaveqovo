Phor Everim - Age, Family, Bio

Tattoo artist at the shop 9Mag who became a cast member on the reality series Black Ink Crew: Chicago.

Before Fame

He grew up on the south side of Chicago, Illinois.

Trivia

He has released a couple of hip hop/rap albums tilted Sacrifice and Lightning Bug with a mononymous stage name of Phor.

Family Life

He dated hair stylist Nikki Nicole.

Associated With

At an Atlanta show, he performed as a rapper on the same bill as Skippa Da Flippa, PnB Rock, Lil Bibby, G Herbo and Cam'ron.
