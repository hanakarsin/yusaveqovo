Moji Olaiya dies two months after giving birth

- Nollywood actress Moji Olaiya is dead

-The mother of two died in Canada where she had gone to have her second child

- Moji gave birth on March 17, 18 years after she had her first daughter

The news of Moji Olaiya's death filtered into the country in the morning of Thursday, May 18. The actress just gave birth to her second child on March 17.

Legit.ng exclusively called one of her closest friends who said she told him she will be crossing to US from Canada where she had her daughter. He said Moji was not sounding sick as at the time they last spoke.
