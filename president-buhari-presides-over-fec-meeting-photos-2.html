President Buhari Presides Over FEC Meeting [Photos]

President Muhammadu Buhari has presided over the Federal Executive Council (FEC) meeting at the State House in Abuja on Wednesday.

The Personal Assistant to the President on Broadcast Media, Buhari Sallau, made this known in a post via his Facebook page.

The caption read, “President Muhammadu Buhari presides over the Federal Executive Council Meeting in State House on 24th Nov 2021.”
