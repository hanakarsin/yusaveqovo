Nancy Leigh Kelley - Age, Family, Bio

Adopted daughter of American film and television actress Katherine Heigl and singer Josh Kelley. Her adopted mother, Katherine, was the Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series award at the 2007 Primetime Emmy Awards ceremony. 

Before Fame

She was adopted by her parents in September of 2009.

Trivia

Her mother was nominated for a Gold Globe award in 2009 due to her performances in the show Grey's Anatomy.

Family Life

She was raised by her adopted family in the United States.

Associated With

Her mother acted alongside Seth Rogen in the 2007 film Knocked Up.
