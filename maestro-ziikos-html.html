Maestro Ziikos - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube star who takes media clips and turns them into a musical compilation that recreates popular music. He found acclaim with the September 2017 YouTube video entitled "Charlie Puth - Attention ( Acapella ) By Trump, Obama, Trudeau, Macron, Clinton ft. Ziikos." He has 2.9 million subscribers on his XhamssaX YouTube account. 

Before Fame

His first YouTube video was entitled "Remix 36 - New Hassan Alfd: divorced Sakhon." It was posted September 2012.

Trivia

His video "Barack Obama Singing Shape of You by Ed Sheeran (VERSION AUTO TUNE) NOW ON iTUNES" surpassed 9 million views on YouTube in 2017. 

Family Life

His real name is Zakaria Chaouch. He is from Rabat, Morocco. 

Associated With

He and La Buscera are both YouTube stars from Morocco.
