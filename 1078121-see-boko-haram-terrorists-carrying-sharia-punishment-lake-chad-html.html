SEE Boko Haram terrorists carrying out Sharia punishment in Lake Chad

The Islamic State formerly known as Boko Haram terrorists have pictures showing its Sharia police in Lake Chad area.

The pictures shows terrorists meting out Sharia punishment to defaulters. The terrorists also claim killing 8 African Coalition Forces in the area.

Years of violence by Boko Haram in Africa’s Lake Chad basin which includes Nigeria, Cameroon, Chad and Niger, has led to a worsening humanitarian crisis that has displaced 1.4 million children and left at least one million still trapped in hard-to-reach areas.

The United Nations Children’s Fund (UNICEF) in a report released in August 2016 said, an estimated 38 children have been used to carry out suicide attacks in the Lake Chad basin region so far this year, bringing to 86 the total number of children used as suicide bombers since 2014.
