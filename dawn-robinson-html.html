Dawn Robinson - Age, Family, Bio

Former member of the dance-pop group En Vogue, the group that released "Don't Let Go (Love)." She was also a singer with the supergroup Lucy Pearl.

Before Fame

She was a founding member of En Vogue in 1989.

Trivia

She has gained reality fame for starring on the TV One series R&B Divas: Atlanta.

Family Life

She was married to Andre Allen from 2003 to 2007. She began dating Christopher Nils Connolly in 2012.

Associated With

She was signed to Dr. Dre's Aftermath Records after leaving Lucy Pearl.
