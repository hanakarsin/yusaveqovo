You Have Changed The Narrative

Aliko Dangote’s Son-in-law, Jamil Mohammed Abubakar has praised Peter Obi supporters for their impact in the upcoming 2023 presidential election.

Sharing his thoughts via a tweet, Abubakar stated that the presidential candidate has changed the narrative surrounding Nigeria’s election.

He stated that Peter Obi’s supporters have garnered so much awareness which has led to a massive number of people obtaining their Permanent Voter’s Card to ensure that their voice is heard in 2023.

He subsequently advised others to stop attacks, and instead focused on selling their candidate.

He wrote: “Whether u like it or not, Peter Obi has changed the narrative on the streets about Nigerian Elections. PVC activations have never been this LOUD! The movement is strong, loud, and aggressive.

“Message: Remember people, Your Vote Counts

“Stop attacks & sell your candidate.”

In other news, Pope Francis has weighed in on the Father Ejike Mbaka’s ‘Peter Obi is stingy’ scandal.

Recall that after Fr Mbaka called out Labor Party flag bearer, Peter Obi for being stingy, the Bishop of Enugu Catholic Diocese, Most Rev. Callistus Onaga ordered Catholic Faithfuls to shun Mbaka’s Adoration ministry.
