The best private primary schools in Nairobi county in 2021

Right after they are born, you start to think about the schools where your child will spend their elementary, primary, and high school years. The school they attend will speak to them in their language and help them become better by the day. For many parents in the Kenyan capital, private primary schools in Nairobi are the best learning environments.

Typically, private primary schools charge a relatively higher amount as school fees compared to their public counterparts. But these institutions offer higher standards of learning to students and unsurprisingly form a more significant chunk of the top primary schools in Nairobi in terms of learning standards and academic performance.

Why parents prefer private primary schools

Parents in Nairobi and other parts of the country prefer private schools for the following reasons:

  • Classroom size – It is common to find public school classrooms filled to capacity. Most private schools offer ample learning space and a healthy teacher to student ratio for the best attention.
  • Motivation to learn – Private institutions strive to be flexible enough to serve each students learning needs to help them manoeuvre the challenging, often tiresome coursework.
  • Amenities – Despite the government's efforts to provide learning resources in public institutions, private schools still stand out due to their excellent amenities for learning.
  • Academic excellence – Most of the best performing primary schools in Nairobi are private schools, highlighting how impactful their excellent quality standards are.
  • Futuristic learning – Most primary schools prepare young scholars for the future. They offer rigorous curricula that also includes additional skills like being proactive, critical thinking and collaboration.

Best private primary schools in Nairobi

The capital city is home to some of the best private primary schools in Kenya. Your choice depends on your budget, proximity to the institution, or the convenience of reaching the school. Other factors include the amenities the school provides and its overall performance in the national examinations.

In no specific order, here is a list of the best private schools in Nairobi county:

Rockfield Junior School

The mixed day and boarding primary school first opened its doors in 1997 with only two pupils in the first week. Over two decades down the line, the institution has grown into one of the best private primary boarding schools. Situated in Tena Estate, off Outering Road, Rockfield Junior School has consistently ranked among the county's top ten in KCPE.

Mirema School

Located in Mirema Drive, off Thika Road, Mirema School was established in 1998 in what seemed like a far-fetched dream of turning a home into a school. The private, coeducational day and boarding school offers a rigorous program, off-campus learning, and a wide range of competitive sports and extracurricular activities.

Mountain View School

Mountain View School was established in 1985 to nurture students into tomorrow's upstanding, disciplined decision-makers. The institution also stimulates curiosity and talent at a tender age and offers every convenience to spark learners' interest in different subjects and extracurricular activities. It is located behind Safari Park, along Thika Super Highway.

Aga Khan Academy

Aga Khan Academy is among the oldest private schools in the county. It was established in 1970 and is located in Nairobi's serene Parklands suburb. The coeducational school is a multi-cultural facility comprising many cultures and nationalities at any given time. The institution's primary objectives are to balance curriculum demands, community life, and sports and cultural activities.

Braeburn Primary School

Braeburn is among the best private primary schools in Westlands in terms of academics and extracurricular activities. The coeducational international institution has students ranging from three to 18 years old and is also among the most expensive. For instance, it offers door to door transport at an annual fee of Ksh. 275,700.

CITAM Schools Buruburu Primary

CITAM Primacy Buruburu is part of Christ Is the Answer ministries located off Jogoo Road within Buruburu Estate, Phase 5. From their Facebook page, the institution boasts offering high quality 8-4-4 and CBC curriculums. The school's rigorous learning program is focused on educating the whole person. Eastland residents can conveniently leverage the amenities and quality of education offered by the school.

Tender Care Academy - Komarock

Komarock's Tender Care Academy is a constituent of three branches serving Umoja, Komarock, and Utawala. The institution is reputable for quality education standards at pre-primary and primary school levels. Parents also prefer the school thanks to the excellent performance track record, qualified and dedicated staff, and a conducive learning environment.

The Arya Vedic School

The primary private school was established in 1980 by Arya Samaj and is located in Ngara. Arya Vedic School has maintained the reputation of academic excellence year after year, hence one of the best primary schools in Nairobi. The primary and secondary education levels follow the IGCSE (International General Certificate of Secondary Education).

Bellevue School

Bellevue School is located in the South C area. The private primary school was established in 1992 and currently boasts more than 8,000 alumni, 700 enrolled students, 37 qualified teachers, and 25 experienced subordinate staff. Students here receive the 8-4-4 education syllabus as well as the CBC program. In 2019, the top three KCPE candidates scored 409, 397, and 396 marks, respectively.

Hillside School

Hillside School, Utawala, prides itself as the ideal facility for child-centred learning that promotes the enthusiasm to study, respect, and excellence in all aspects of life. The school also promises to foster personal and academic growth for your child to make them responsible citizens. This is true if the 2019 KCPE results are anything to go by. The school attained an impressive mean score of 386 out of 500.

The list of the best private primary schools in Nairobi county is not only limited to these. More and more institutions are doing their best to offer the best setup for children growth in all aspects of life. With the main consideration factors in mind and a detailed overview of the best alternatives, you can now make an informed choice.
