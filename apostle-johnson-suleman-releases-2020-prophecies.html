Apostle Johnson Suleman Releases 2020 Prophecies

The General Overseer of Omega Fire Ministries (OFM), Apostle Johnson Suleman, has released prophecies for the year 2020.

Naija News reports that the OFM General Overseer released the prophecies on the micro-blogging site, Twitter on Wednesday morning, January 1, 2020, New Year’s Day.

This online news platform understands that Apostle Suleman prayed in his 2020 prophecies that this “year 2020 shall be your year of greater glory and greater works.”

Apostle Suleman who has been tagged by as “controversial” in some quarters, prayed that those that believe the 2020 prophecies released by him, “will become an institution.”

Stressing the importance of the scripture, Apostle Suleman quoted John 16.20 and prayed that “sorrow shall be turned to joy” for those that believe the 2020 prophecies and that they shall enjoy peace on every side.

Apostle Suleman prayed that this year, helpers will come to the believers of the 2020 prophecies released by him from everywhere.

Below are Apostle Johnson Suleman’s 2020 prophecies…

“This year 2020 shall be your year of greater glory and greater works…

“You will become an institution…

“Your sorrow shall be turned to joy(John 16.20)…

“You shall enjoy peace on every side..helpers will come to you from everywhere in Jesus name..happy new year.”
