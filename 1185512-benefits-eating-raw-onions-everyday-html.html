8 Top benefits of eating raw onions every day explained

How useful is eating onions 7 days a week? What are the benefits of eating raw onions every day? Let us explore the magic advantages of this vegetable. You will be impressed by how useful it is for your body.

Eating raw onions

Onion is a popular vegetable all over the world. It grows all year round, and it is a flavour element for most dishes. Nigerians, for example, love to add white, green, yellow and red onions to their dishes because this vegetable adds a unique flavour and makes you healthier.

If cooked onions can have a sweet taste and nice flavour, eating raw onions can sound like a problem at first. Still, the raw vegetables provide you with more vitamins and useful elements that is why it is better to eat them raw.

Health benefits of onions

We will list some of the top health benefits of eating onions for men and women you should know about.

1. Stronger immune system

Eating raw onions can be useful for your immune system. This useful product enriches our bodies with antioxidants and creates a ‘wall’ to protect you from free radicals.

Add more onions to your meals and salads and your immune system will easier fight different bacteria and viruses. In other words, you will feel much better than most people who do not like onions. You will not catch the flu as often as others and you will recover quicker.

Is this really possible? How? We know the answers. According to doctors, each raw onion has quercetin. This plant flavonol helps the human body fight allergies and cold symptoms such as sneezing.

2. Healthier heart

Do you want your heart to be healthier and stronger? This is one of the most important raw onion health benefits. Experts believe that raw onion lowers the risks of heart and heart attacks.

Onion juice influences your blood pressure, reduces it and works as prevention to platelets and clusters. There are different studies on the influence of onion on the human heart, and some of them suggest that this vegetable has a positive effect. Each onion you eat helps your blood regulation, improves cholesterol levels and makes the heart stronger.

3. Easier breathing

Onions help to relax your muscles, and this can result in easier breathing. For example, if you have asthma, you might ease its symptoms if you eat raw onions every day. However, it is extremely important for every patient with asthma to talk to their doctor before trying to eat onions as a treatment against such condition.

4. Improved digestion

The cool benefits of eating onions for men and women include improved digestive system. Thanks to fibre contained in the vegetable, onions are great for better digestion.

By the way, when you start eating raw onions, you will get enough fibre for the development of the right bacteria in your body. Human intestines need the right bacteria for better functioning and prevent different problems such as diarrhoea or stomach ulcer.

5. Stronger bones

It doesn’t matter if you are young or getting older, you should take care of your health. Eating vegetables is great for your body because they all are an amazing natural source of vitamins.

Onions are rich in calcium which is a necessary element for the bones. Those people who consume these vegetables in the raw state easily reduce the possibilities of bone fractures by about twenty percent. This is really nice, so think about adding this vegetable to your family meals.

6. Reduction of blood sugar

Raw onion health benefits include the control of blood sugars. The elements contained in red onions, for example, can help with reducing the level of glucose for a while.

In other words, if you have diabetes you can talk to your doctor about adding onions to your everyday meals.

7. Improved hair and skin

Those women and men who take care of their skin and wish to enjoy healthier hair have noticed how onion consumption helps with improving their skin and hair.

8. Antiseptic benefit

You already know that raw onions can make your immune system more powerful, but this is not the only advantage of consuming these impressive vegetables.

If you have a mouth infection, you can use onion as your natural antibiotic. Just cut raw onions into slices and eat with other vegetables or bread or alone. You can also add onions to salads and eat it fresh with a little bit of vegetable oil.
