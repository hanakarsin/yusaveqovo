Prettyboiballo - Age, Family, Bio

Social media star who posts lip-sync and comedy videos to his prettyboiballo TikTok account. He often posts comedy skits in which he impersonates two different characters. His videos have earned him over 4.7 million followers on the app. 

Before Fame

He was a member of his high school football team. 

Trivia

His first TikTok to reach over 1 million views was a lip-sync video about a girl trying to get his attention. 

Family Life

Tony Caraballo is his real name. His mother has appeared in videos on his TikTok account. He has one older brother and one younger brother.

Associated With

He posted a duet with TikTok star Olivia Ponton in May 2020.
