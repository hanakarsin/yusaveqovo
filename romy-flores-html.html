Romy Flores - Age, Family, Bio

A.K.A. DeathByRomy, singer/songwriter and rapper who brings a goth sensibility to all of her work. She released her first full-length album Monsters in September of 2018 and she's been managed by Alec Hendo. 

Before Fame

She's been based in Los Angeles. 

Trivia

Hendo discovered her by listening to her covers of Bjork's "Venus."

Family Life

She is of Mexican and European descent. 

Associated With

She's friends with Adam Grandmaison a.k.a. Adam 22 and the two have appeared together on each other's Instagram.
