Martha Hunt - Age, Family, Bio

Victoria Secret fashion model who walked the runway at the Victoria's Secret Fashion Show 2013 and was part of their Swim Special in 2015. She also became a brand ambassador for the apparel line Free People.

Before Fame

A photographer discovered her in Charlotte, NC and introduced her to modeling agencies. Soon after, she signed with IMG Models.

Trivia

Her first runway walk came in 2007 at Paris Fashion Week.

Family Life

She was born in Wilson, North Carolina. On November 6, 2021, she welcomed a daughter named Emery with her fiancé Jason McDonald. They got engaged in 2020.

Associated With

She has walked for numerous top brands, including but not limited to Oscar de la Renta, Chanel, Dolce & Gabbana, Giorgio Armani, Jason Wu, Louis Vuitton, Prada, Rag & Bone, Ralph Lauren and Versace.
