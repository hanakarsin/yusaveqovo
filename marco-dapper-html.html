Marco Dapper - Age, Family, Bio

Actor and model known for playing Troy in Eating Out 2: Sloppy Seconds and Carmine Basco on The Young and the Restless. He's also appeared in episodes of 90210 and Criminal Minds.

Before Fame

After moving to Los Angeles in 2003, he worked various odd jobs, including at a UPS warehouse and as a waiter, while studying acting.

Trivia

He's modeled internationally for Levi's Jeans and has appeared in a variety of commercials for brands like Absolut Vodka and Xbox. He's also a passionate painter.

Family Life

He was raised Catholic and attended catholic school growing up. His older sister, Michelle, is a sports reporter.

Associated With

He appeared in an episode of Veronica Mars, which starred Kristen Bell. He was officially credited as "Super Hot Guy."
