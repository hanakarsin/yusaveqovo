Toyin Abraham Consoles Nkechi Blessing As She Loses Mother

The mother of popular Nollywood actress, Nkechi Blessing, Gloria Sunday, has reportedly died.

According to Toyin Abraham, via her Instagram page, the vibrant actress mother, Gloria Sunday died on Thursday morning.

She also urged the general public to put the actress’ family in prayers as they mourn their dead. 

Announcing the sad news, she wrote; “We just lost @nkechiblessingsunday MUM. Please put us in your prayers.“

Naija News recalls that Nkechi Blessing gave her mother a surprise on mother’s day.

Sharing the photos, her mother was seen happily holding a box of money that was used to spell the word MUM.

Captioning the photos, the 32 year old wrote; “No long Cap Mummy Just Relax and continue to Reap the fruit of your labour Main gift will be in few weeks I love you piece AFIANMA Akwa Ibom Finest…Shout out to my surest plug @royalhugssurprises for always coming Thru for me and to every mother’s in the house may you reap the fruit of your labour Isu Omo Ajina fun yin Je loruko Jesu#motherhood…

“As long as you have all you need without stressing, that is my biggest Flex in life Happy Mother’s Day IYA NKECHI 1 of the universe”
