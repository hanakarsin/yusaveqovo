It Was A Silly Mistake, I Don't Want My Parents To See It Netizen Who Claims To Be Woman In James

A lady who claims to be the unidentified female in the s3x tape of popular Nigerian cross dresser and comedian, James Brown has asked netizens to stop sharing the video.

Naija News recalls that on Monday, social media was agog after a video of the crossdresser getting intimate with a lady surfaced online.

The clip stirred a lot of reactions from netizens who shared their thoughts regarding the tape.

Reacting to the tape, James had said that he was depressed and had no idea how it got leaked.

In a recent development, a lady has boldly come out on social media to say she is the one on the tape while begging Nigerians to stop sharing the video.

According to her, she mistakenly posted it and does not want her parents to see the the clip.

Harrison Calls For Prayers As He Secures Freedom

Nigerian singer, Harrison Okiri, popularly known as Harrysong, has regained freedom from Police custody, days after his arrest.

Naija News reports that the singer took to his Instagram page on Wednesday night to announce his release, stating that he is perfectly in good condition.

Harrysong also promised to share details of their settlement and agreement soon.

He wrote: “I’m out & Ok. As I no die”. Were good now. Details of our agreement & settlement”

In a subsequent post, the singer urged his fans to pray for him.

In his word: “Pls say a prayer for me. Where ever you are. Just a prayer, with love”
