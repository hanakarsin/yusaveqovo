Air Force Officer Arrested Over Nigerian Defence Academy Attack

Security operatives on Monday arrested Nigerian Air Force officer, Sergeant Torsobo Solomon over with the recent attack on the Nigerian Defence Academy (NDA), Kaduna.

Naija News recalls that bandit terrorists had on August 24 attacked the NDA killing two officers and abducting another who was later released.

The suspect boarding a plane

The officer who was arrested in Yola, Adamawa state capital is currently attached to the Air Force Comprehensive School, Yola.

A source disclosed that Solomon was arrested with arms and ammunition following an order of the Base Commander as requested by the NDA Commander for an offence connected with the recent attack on NDA, Kaduna.

Sergeant Torsobo Solomon

The source said: “On 08 Nov 21, at about 0600hr, NAF07/ 23922, SGT Torsobo Solomon of 153 BSG (AIRFORCE COMPREHENSIVE SCHOOL YOLA) was arrested on the order of the Base Commander as requested by the NDA Commander for an offence connecting with arms and ammunition with recent bandit attack at NDA Kaduna.

“At about 0815hr, ATR with reg no NAF 930 to arrive flight line to convey him from Yola to NDA KADUNA for further interrogation.”

“Below is the suspect onboard the ATR ac to Kaduna.”
