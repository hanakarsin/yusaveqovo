Chrissy Rasmussen - Age, Family, Bio

Social media star and hairstylist who is famous for her self-titled YouTube channel. She has gained massive popularity for her hairstyling tips and tutorials alongside hand-made extension showcases. She became the founder of her own studio called Habit Salon.

Before Fame

She began her YouTube channel in February 2014. 

Trivia

She served as a global brand ambassador for the haircare companies Olaplex & Goldwell. She also owned the Habit Salon. 

Family Life

She is based in Arizona. She sometimes posts Instagram photos featuring her family dog Cody.

Associated With

She and Cole LaBrant have been close personal friends and frequently featured together on social media.
