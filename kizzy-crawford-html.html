Kizzy Crawford - Age, Family, Bio

Known simply as Kizzy, she rose to fame in 2013 with the album The Starling. She is known for fusing her Welsh and Bajan heritage and often sings in both English and Welsh.

Before Fame

In 2012, she won the Arts Connect Original Singer-Songwriter prize.

Trivia

She has a self-titled YouTube channel where she has posted original music videos.

Family Life

She has a younger sister and a younger brother.

Associated With

She worked with Amy Wadge on her debut single, "Starling."
