Ia Ostergren - Age, Family, Bio

Fitness model and bodybuilder who showcases her results and pictures from her modeling shoots on a self-titled Instagram account with 200,000 followers. 

Before Fame

She began documenting her already present fitness journey on Instagram in August of 2014. 

Trivia

In addition to documenting her fitness journey online, she used Instagram to document her body journey while pregnant. 

Family Life

She married Swedish national champion bodybuilder Torbjörn Östergren. They have two daughters together. 

Associated With

She and Ako Rahim both became Swedish fitness models.
